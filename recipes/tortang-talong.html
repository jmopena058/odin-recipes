Tortang Talong Recipe

Learn how to Cook special Tortang Talong in a few simple steps

This delicious, classic recipe is a perfect go-to in most Filipino households. This is because it is quick, easy, and best of all, absolutely tasty without sacrificing nutritional value.

Tortang Talong Recipe Ingredients

  • 2 pcs Fresh Eggs
  • Garlic(minced)
  • Diced Onion
  • Eggplant
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Follow these simple steps to make your Delicious Tortang Talonggis

  1. Grill or Boil Eggplants
  2. Mix Fresh eggs, diced onion and minced garlic into a bowl.
  3. Add ground pepper and salt(to taste)
  4. Peel and remove the skin of Boiled/Grilled Egpplant
  5. Soak the Eggplant to the Egg mixtures
  6. Fry the Soaked Eggplant and wait till it turn golden-brown
  7. Serve and Enjoy it till next life!
