Tortang Talong Recipe

Learn how to Cook special Tortang Talong in a few simple steps

This delicious, classic recipe is a perfect go-to in most Filipino households. This is because it is quick, easy, and best of all, absolutely tasty without sacrificing nutritional value.

Tortang Talong Recipe Ingredients

  • 2 pcs Fresh Eggs
  • Garlic(minced)
  • Diced Onion
  • Eggplant
  • Salt
  • Ground Pepper
