How to Cook Tinola

Tinola one of the most common Filipino Dishes

Tinola is one of the most delicious and popular Pinoy dishes. It is a soup with a Chicken and mixed vegetables that makes it healthy and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken(tinola cut)
  • Chayote(sayote)/Papaya
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fish sauce/paste
  • Black Pepper
  • Moringa Leaves/Malungay leaves
  • Chilli Pepper

Procedure:

  1. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger
  2. Put-in the chicken and cook until color turns light brown
  3. Add the fish sauce(to taste), and mix well
  4. Add water and put to boil. Simmer it for 45 minutes
  5. Add the green papaya wedges and simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Add Moringa leaves
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste>
  8. Serve and andjoy! ^,^
